Key services

Designed for producers, agro-exporters, DFIs, funds, banks, importers and retailers seeking ESG rigor with financial and operational focus.

ESG • Due diligence • ESMS

ESG assessments, due diligence and design of Environmental & Social Management Systems (ESMS) aligned with IFC Performance Standards.

We help prepare borrowers and companies to access responsible finance.

Sustainable finance

Structuring of frameworks, KPIs and impact models for bonds, loans and sustainable taxonomies.

We connect ESG performance with financial metrics and investment criteria.

Monitoring • Reporting • Data

Implementation of sustainability measurement and verification processes combining satellite monitoring, on-site verification and data dashboards.

We support annual reports and investor disclosures.

We develop information systems and web dashboards that integrate operational, environmental and financial data, enabling KPI tracking and preparation of ESG & impact reports.
